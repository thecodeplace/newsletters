About IAF

Facilitation is a profession rapidly taking its unique place alongside those of consulting and training. The International Association of Facilitation (www.iaf-world.org) was formed by a group of professionals designing an avenue for interchange, professional development, trend analysis and peer networking. A formal association was proposed and adopted at a networking conference in Alexandria, Virginia, in January, 1994.More than 70 people signed on as charter members. Since then the IAF has grown to over 1500 members in more than 63 countries. Our members work in government, nonprofit, educational, community and corporate environments and hold positions as consultants, teacher, in-house facilitators, negotiators, organizational specialists, coaches among others.

About the Conference

After 3 years of collaborating, weaving stories and facilitating worlds across China, Japan and Singapore the 18th Annual IAF Asia Facilitators Conference returns to the historical, colourful and always bustling Mumbai in India. The IAF Asia Facilitators Conference to be held at Vivanta by Taj - President, Mumbai is a fantastic opportunity to meet and learn from colleagues in companies and organisations with the same desire to get inspiration, tools, ideas and connections in the area of facilitation. The conference provides every participant the possibility to learn, share and engage in the art of facilitation.The theme of the 18th Annual IAF Asia Facilitators Conference “Facilitation Unplugged : Exploring the power of facilitation“ focuses on exploring and expanding the power of facilitation across all types of organizations. The conference aims to help participants explore the power of facilitation across three tracks:

Discover Track:

Come and “discover” what facilitation is and the enormous power process facilitation provides through simple tools and processes.

Engage Track:

Come and explore the processes that create “engage”ment through facilitation particularly in areas such as facilitating multicultural teams, leveraging conflict for inclusion, or other strategies.

Innovate Track

Come and experience the Innovative facilitation processes and tools that are unique, new, different, and creatively applied in various scenarios that are being showcased for the first time in India.
